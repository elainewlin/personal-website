Software. Music. Food.

Music + Software = MusicParsed

I started learning guitar in February 2016, but switched to playing ukulele for several reasons. While looking up song chords to play, I found existing online sites hard to use - it was difficult to track all songs you play, quickly load different songs, and play through a song without interruption. I sing, play ukulele, and code web apps, so I built MusicParsed, a fast, open source site for song chords.

Food + Software = FoodParsed

The tagline for my food blog, FoodParsed, used to be "Cooking with minimal ingredients". I used to have orthorexia, an unhealthy obsession with healthy eating, and many earlier posts are absurdly detailed. Now, I changed the tagline to "A healthy approach to healthy eating," and I write about food and mental health.
