Can you refer me to Figma?

If you would like a referral, please email me your resume, a link to the role you are interested in, and a short blurb on why you’re a good fit for the role. You do not need to also apply for the role - the referral is your application. Note that you do not have to meet every single requirement to be qualified for a role.

If you are newer to tech, you are also welcome to reach out. You may also find it helpful to read my post on finding a speciality in software engineering.
