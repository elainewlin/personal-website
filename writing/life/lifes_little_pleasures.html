life's little pleasures

When life feels tedious, I need to remind myself of life’s little pleasures. I wrote mine below. What are yours?

living

  • Sleeping in fresh clean sheets
  • Waking up after a nap
  • Feeling a fluffy blanket
  • Hugging a stuffed plush

drinking

  • Making foamy drinks at home
  • Waking up with a hot cup of coffee
  • Drinking cool water when you’re thirsty

walking

  • Staring at people’s pooches as they pass
  • Seeing a racoon or skunk on a walk
  • Watching the sunrise from the top of a hill

listening

  • Bopping to beats on a walk
  • Listening to a trashy podcast
  • Remembering an old favorite song

creating

  • Learning a new song on the uke
  • Figuring out a tricky strum pattern or riff
  • Reading a nice comment from an Internet stranger

cooking

  • Prepping ingredients in neat little bowls
  • Baking fresh bread, cookies, and cakes
  • Sauteeing onions for soup
  • Taking the first bite of a new dish

eating

  • Sitting down with a meal when you’re hungry
  • Savoring a piece of dark chocolate
  • Biting into a tasty dessert
  • Opening your cabinet and finding your favorite snack
